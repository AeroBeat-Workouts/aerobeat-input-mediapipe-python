## Investigation Topics

### 1. Build Distribution Strategy
**Goal:** Make `aerobeat-assembly-community` installable on a fresh PC without any dependencies

**Document:** [`INVESTIGATION-build-distribution.md`](./INVESTIGATION-build-distribution.md)

**Key Approaches:**
- Full Bundle (all-in-one with embedded Python)
- Installer with Auto-Download
- PyInstaller single executable
- Docker/Container (dev only)

---

### 2. User Camera View
**Goal:** Show the actual camera feed alongside tracking dots

**Document:** [`INVESTIGATION-camera-view.md`](./INVESTIGATION-camera-view.md)

**Key Approaches:**
- Python streams MJPEG to Godot over UDP
- v4l2loopback virtual camera (Linux)
- Separate camera instances
- Overlay-only (landmarks only)
- Hybrid toggle system (recommended)

**Research Finding:** Production apps treat camera view as a **calibration tool**, not permanent gameplay UI.

---

### 3. Latency Reduction
**Goal:** Reduce delay between human movement and tracking dot response

**Document:** [`INVESTIGATION-latency-reduction.md`](./INVESTIGATION-latency-reduction.md)

**Key Approaches:**
- MediaPipe Lite model (`model_complexity=0`)
- Reduce camera buffer and resolution
- Binary serialization (struct vs JSON)
- Frame skipping with interpolation
- Threaded pipeline architecture

**Expected Improvement:** 50-150ms → 30-60ms (2-4 frames @ 60fps)
